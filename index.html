About me

Hello, I'm Olah choice

Hi, I am Olah Choice, a product designer.
I hail from Cross River State Nigeria, I studied health
education and transitioned to product design with my
most prominent skill of breaking down the most complex
problems to come up with the simplest solution that
benefits everyone.

I love designing, most precisely, I love carrying out research, documenting practical actionable insights, creating information architectures, ideating, creating UIs, and finally testing my products.

I understand the importance of experimentation when it comes to design and applying the right strategies that resonate with the desired business goals can have a huge impact that would make users happy.
